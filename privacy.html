Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Kamra Community Hub is a community platform for members of the Pakistan Aeronautical Complex (PAC) Kamra.

Kamra Hub is an independent, unofficial community app. It is not operated by, endorsed by, or affiliated with Pakistan Aeronautical Complex (PAC) or any government entity.

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Community posts
  • Announcements
  • Images uploaded by users
  • Device information for app functionality

Authentication

Users sign in using email and password.

Photos

Users may upload profile pictures and post images.

Advertising

Kamra Community Hub may use Google AdMob to display ads.

Location

Kamra Community Hub does not collect GPS or precise location.

Storage

Data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database and file storage), with row-level security restricting access to authorized users only.

Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect user data.
