Child Safety Standards

Effective Date: July 2026

Kamra Community Hub is a private, independent community platform intended for use by adults (18 and older) living in Kamra and surrounding areas, and their adult family members. Kamra Community Hub is not operated by, endorsed by, or affiliated with any government entity or organization. Kamra Community Hub does not knowingly permit children to create accounts.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Kamra Community Hub has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) material of any kind. Any account found to be involved in creating, sharing, or requesting such content will be immediately and permanently banned, and reported to the relevant authorities and to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or equivalent local authorities where applicable.

Prevention Measures

  • Every account requires a verified, active email address before use.
  • The target audience for Kamra Community Hub is adults (18+); the app is not directed at or marketed to children.
  • Private messaging automatically blocks attempts to share phone numbers or email addresses, reducing opportunities for off-platform contact.

In-App Reporting

Kamra Community Hub allows users to report any post or piece of content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed by the app administrator. Any content involving child sexual abuse material will be removed immediately upon discovery and escalated to appropriate law enforcement authorities.

Compliance

Kamra Community Hub complies with all applicable child safety laws and cooperates fully with regional and national authorities regarding any reports of CSAE.
